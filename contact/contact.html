The Bluegrass, Cumberlands, Northern Kentucky and South Central Workforce Development/Innovation Boards are launching "Commonwealth Coders," an intensive 16-week Web Development Training Course again in January 2022. While this is the first step toward becoming a student in the program, it is an important one. Please make your very best effort as spots will be limited.
